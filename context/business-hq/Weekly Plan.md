# Weekly Plan

Week of: 2026-06-01

## Theme

Create the minimum business operating system needed to get paid, get clear, and start moving toward more clients.

## Top Outcomes

1. Clarify first-payment setup and LLC urgency.
2. Describe the current consulting work in plain language.
3. Draft the first version of the business offer.
4. Identify the first administrative tasks.
5. Decide what a simple website needs to say.

## This Week's Actions

### Business Setup

- Confirm payment timeline with current client.
- Ask whether they need a W-9, LLC, EIN, certificate of insurance, vendor form, or contract.
- Prepare questions for CPA or attorney about LLC timing, tax treatment, and first payment.
- Choose 2-3 candidate LLC names.
- Decide whether to form the LLC before the first invoice.
- If forming now, file Articles of Organization, then get EIN and open business banking.

### Offer Definition

- Write a plain-language description of the work being done for the current client.
- List the outcomes the client actually cares about.
- Identify which parts of the work could become a repeatable offer.

### Pipeline

- Make an initial list of 10 people or companies who might need similar help.
- Note why each might care now.

### Brand / Website

- Draft a one-sentence positioning statement.
- Draft a short bio.
- Draft a simple website outline.

- Complex automation
- Detailed long-range business plan
